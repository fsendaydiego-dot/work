Layer 4 — Distribution & Implementation
AXIS: VERTICAL · Defines WHERE in the value chain delivery occurs across all segments

Critical Intersection

Where Segment 8 meets Layer 4 — The Agency Reseller Sweet Spot

The single cell where the Autonomous Voice Agent horizontal slice crosses the Distribution & Implementation vertical column is the precise location of the Voice AI Agency Reseller (AAA). This intersection is where Vapi and Synthflow get white-labeled, where SMB clients get served, and where the $1.30B agency channel earns its revenue by making $2.70B worth of Segment 8 technology accessible to the long tail of the market. Neither axis is more important than the other — the agency reseller only exists because both lines cross. Remove Segment 8 and there is nothing to resell. Remove Layer 4 and Segment 8 cannot reach SMB at scale. The intersection is the entire business model.
